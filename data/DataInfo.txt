----------- DATA IN -------------

FREQUENCY DATA, data_freq 2025:
0.1 second resolution, Finland, Sweden and DK2 operate in the same synchronous grid
so the activation values apply directly to DK2.
Source: https://data.fingrid.fi/en/datasets/339
Processed by: data_processing/1_process_frequency.py
Output:       data_out/frequency_activation_factors_2025.csv
Method: hourly FCR-D and FCR-N activation fractions computed from the share of
seconds per hour in which frequency leaves the normal band, using the piecewise
linear formulas in Crowley et al. (2024).

FCRD PRICE DATA, data_FCRD 2025:
Hourly auction prices for FCR-D up/down and FCR-N (DK2), EUR/MW/h.
Source: https://www.energidataservice.dk/tso-electricity/FcrNdDK2
Processed by: data_processing/1b_process_fcrd.py
Unit conversion: EUR/MW/h × 7.46 (DKK/EUR) ÷ 10 = øre/kWh

SPOT PRICE DATA, data_spot 2025:
Hourly resolution for the first part of 2025 (Elspotprices dataset):
  https://www.energidataservice.dk/tso-electricity/Elspotprices
15-minute resolution for the second part of 2025 (DayAheadPrices dataset):
  https://energidataservice.dk/tso-electricity/DayAheadPrices
A shift from hourly to 15-minute resolution occurred during 2025; both sources
are combined and averaged to hourly in step 0_process_spot_ec.ipynb.

ENERGY COMMUNITY DATA, data_ec 2025:
Per-meter hourly energy consumption and PV production for two Danish energy
communities ('b' and 's'), provided by Enyday.com.
Processed by: data_processing/0_process_spot_ec.ipynb

WEATHER DATA:
Fetched from Open-Meteo API via data_processing/_process_weather.ipynb /
config.py. Downloaded but not used in the final study.

----------- DATA PROCESSING PIPELINE --------

Step 0 — data_processing/0_process_spot_ec.ipynb
  Reads  : data_in/data_spot/Elspotprices.csv
           data_in/data_spot/DayAheadPrices.csv
           data_in/data_ec/  (b_data and s_data CSVs)
  Writes : data_out/spot_prices_2025_complete.csv
           data_out/combined_2025.csv
             (one row per hour × ec_id, with spot + buy/sell prices, EC load/PV)

Step 1 — data_processing/1_process_frequency.py   [heavy, skip if output exists]
  Reads  : data_in/data_freq/  (monthly Fingrid 0.1-second CSV files)
  Writes : data_out/frequency_activation_factors_2025.csv

Step 1b — data_processing/1b_process_fcrd.py
  Reads  : data_in/data_FCRD/FcrNdDK2.csv
           data_out/combined_2025.csv
  Writes : data_out/combined_2025_clean.csv
             (adds 9 FCRD price columns in øre/kWh)

Step 2 — data_processing/2_combine_hourly_2025.py
  Reads  : data_out/combined_2025_clean.csv
           data_out/frequency_activation_factors_2025.csv
  Writes : data_out/combined_2025_with_frequency.csv  ← FINAL DATASET

Step 3 — data_processing/3_check_combined_frequency.ipynb
  Validation notebook; reads combined_2025_with_frequency.csv and reports
  column completeness and NaN counts.

----------- DATA OUT ------------

combined_2025.csv                      — step 0 output (spot + EC data)
combined_2025_clean.csv                — step 1b output (adds FCRD prices)
frequency_activation_factors_2025.csv  — step 1 output (hourly activation factors)
combined_2025_with_frequency.csv       — FINAL dataset used by notebooks
backtest_upper_bound_2025.csv          — result output from 01_Back_test_6_LER.ipynb
individual_day_results.csv             — result output from 01_Back_test_6_LER.ipynb
